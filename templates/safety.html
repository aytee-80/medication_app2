⚠️ Medication Overdose & Addiction Warning

Never take more than the prescribed dosage. Misuse can result in serious health consequences.

🚫 Risks of Overdose

  • 💥 Organ damage (liver, kidneys)
  • ⚠️ Severe health complications or death
  • 🔀 Dangerous drug interactions

🧠 Addiction & Dependency

  • 🔒 Habit-forming potential
  • 🕒 Long-term misuse risks
  • 📋 Always follow doctor’s instructions

👨‍⚕️ Always Consult Your Doctor

This app helps manage your prescriptions, but it does not replace professional medical advice. Always talk to your healthcare provider before changing medication routines.

🚨 Emergency Instructions

If you or someone you know is experiencing a suspected overdose, follow these steps immediately:

  • 📞 Call emergency services: 0800 123 456
  • 🧍‍♂️ Keep the person awake and sitting upright
  • 🚑 Stay with them until help arrives
  • 💬 Tell emergency responders what medications were taken

⚠️ Never delay in seeking help during an overdose situation.
